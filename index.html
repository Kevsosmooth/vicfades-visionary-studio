Meet Vic

Vic isn't just cutting hair -- he's chasing a vision. Years behind the chair, thousands of fades later, and every cut still gets the same precision and respect as the first. That's the standard.

But what makes Vic different is what he does outside the chair. Free back-to-school haircuts for kids in Lakewood. Free cuts for the homeless. Mentoring aspiring barbers. He's not just building a business -- he's building a community.
